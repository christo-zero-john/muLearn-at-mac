μlearn Mar Augusthinose College Campus Chapter

Democratise Decentralise Digitize Learning

μlearn is established on the firm foundation of the research findings in educational sciences and education psychology over the past several decades to democratize knowledge through knowledge co-creation. We aim to create a platform that is made of several "affinity spaces" where real learning takses place.

Inspired by the silicon valley movement, μlearn aims to foster an enviornment that nurtures groundbreaking ideas and drives technological advancements.


μlearn is an innovative hub dedicated to future technologies, with the vision of transforming kerala into a knowledge-based, sustainable, and innovation centric state.
